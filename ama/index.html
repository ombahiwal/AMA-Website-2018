    About Us

    Aurangabad Management Association (AMA) was founded in the year 1977 by a group of enthusiastic entrepreneurs and management professionals with the mission "To undertake path-breaking management initiatives in sync with the timeless principle that sharing brings abundance".

    Our Vision

    To undertake Path-Breaking Management Initiatives in sync with the timeless principle that "Sharing Brings Abundance".

    Our Mission

    To undertake path-breaking management initiatives in sync with the timeless principle that sharing brings abundance.



    Objectives of AMA

    • To develop culturally rooted and innovative Management in the region holistically.

    • To promote professional management education through its programs and initiatives.

    • To enhance and upgrade managerial acumen and practices.

    • To provide a platform for sharing ideas and exchanging knowledge and experiences on trends in the field of management in keeping with the changing global environment.

    • To promote and develop sound management principles mixing best of both, the Indian and international environment.

    • To establish a fruitful liaison for its members with the various management institutions, both in India and abroad.




    History of AMA

    Aurangabad Management Association (AMA) was founded in the year 1977 by a group of enthusiastic entrepreneurs and management professionals.The AMA programs encompass a wide spectrum of audience involving practicing managers, industries, students of management, faculty and the management schools of Aurangabad. AMA is committed to enhance & upgrade managerial acumen and practices.

    Aurangabad is one of the fastest growing cities in Asia. Many multinationals and established corporate's have their units and offices in Aurangabad. The industrial development of Aurangabad is attracting many international companies to set up their ventures. The Software Technology Park, Auto cluster, upcoming Mumbai-Delhi industrial corridor in the city have added to Aurangabad's importance as an industrial hub. All this development calls for an international management mind set. It unerlines the need for competence at international level and professional attitude different from the traditional one. AMA strives to meet these challenges and suffice the need through its programs and interventions.

    AMA's flagship event Rare Share has become popular with the management fraternity of Aurangabad. The program is attended in large numbers by managers, management institutions, industries, student and faculty. To achieve its objective of providing a platform for sharing ideas and exchanging knowledge and experience on trends in the field of management in keeping with the changing environment, the idea of Rare Share emerged. Eminent achievers from diverse fields and cities have been invited to address the people of Aurangabad to share their journey and success factors. Each month the earnest talks given by speakers create an environment of admiration at the event.

    Events

    At AMA the management development events provide a continuing educational avenue for member, employees of member organizations, entrepreneurs and students. AMA provides a regular forum for members adn other professionals to meet, share and enrich their experiences.

    MoU with Project Management Associates

    MoU with Project Management Associates A MoU was signed between AMA and Project Management Associates with the main intent of creating a "Project Oriented", Aurangabad.

    Seminar By Mr. Adesh Jain

    A high level seminar was conducted by the President of Project Management Associates, ADESH JAIN, for the benefit of the industry and faculty of the region. Mr. Adesh Jain is an authority on Project Management at an international level and is India's first IPMA A and PMP certified professional.

    Project Management Course

    A three day holistic program on "Certificate in Project Management Course" was conducted by PMA, for the first time in Aurangabad. This is equivalent to IPMA-D level certification, the basic step in becoming a project management expert.


    Young Managers Contest

    The aim was to provide a platform for young, talented and budding managers in Aurangabad region to prepare themselves for participating in the national level competition by All India Management Association. The theme for the contest was "Inspired Leadership for turbulent times and the power of youth". The team of Endress + Hauser won the contest.

    Seminar by Dr. Devdutt Pattanaik

    As is belief, so is behavior, so is business. It is aptly put by Dr. Pattanaik that if we believe that wealth needs to be chased, the workplace becomes a rana-bhoomi battleground of investors, employers, employees, competitors and customers; if we believe that wealth needs to be attracted, the workplace becomes a rang-bhoomi playground where everyone is happy.

    Session by Mr. Pradipta Mahapatra

    Mr. Pradipta Mahapatra conducted an exclusive session on CEO Coaching and its importance in family managed businesses. He is a Harvard Business School alumnus. He is also the founder of CFI and Chennai Business School.

    Dare to Dream

    This one day long DARE TO DREAM - Leadership summit sought to challenge core beliefs of the audience in search of deeper truth, while celebrating the thinkers, dreamers and mavericks who dared to offer bold new alternatives to grow beyond limitations and fly high. The event comprised of three sessions conducted by eminent speakers.

    Magazine - PRABANDHAN

    Two editions of the magazine PRABANDHAN have been published and distributed across Aurangabad focusing on sharing of management principles, inspirational stories and thoughts while giving updates on several events of AMA.
